# Definitive List of Chuck Arnett Works Donated to the Archive

Based on the Arnett Exhibit 2014 records and archival drafts.

## Artworks & Posters

2. **"HOMOSEXUALITY IN AMERICA," Life: June 26, 1964**

4. **"God Bless Walt Disney"**

9. **Four of Wands (Four of Giant Dildos)**

10. **Ace of Swords (Ace of Points)**

12. **Haight Street Free Clinic benefit poster**
